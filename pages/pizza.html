Pizza

Description

This pepperoni pizza recipe produces a quick and easy classic! Delicious homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce have never been easier.

Today, we're making a delicious pepperoni pizza. We start with fresh dough, add tomato sauce and plenty of melted mozzarella cheese. The star of the show? Thinly sliced pepperoni! It's spicy, salty, and savory. After baking to perfection, you'll enjoy a mouthwatering pizza that's hard to resist.

Ingredients

For the base

  • 300g strong bread flavour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling

For the tomato sauce

  • 100ml passata
  • handful fresh basil or 1tsp dried
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed

    • For the topping

    • 125g ball mozzarella, sliced
    • handful grated or shaved parmesan or vegetarian alternative
    • handful of cherry tomatoes, halved

    To finish

    • handful of basil leaves (optional)
    • Secret odin sauce, only available if you join the discord!(Im joking ofc)

    Steps

    1. To make the base, mix flour, yeast, and salt in a big bowl. Create a well, add 200ml warm water and olive oil. Stir until it forms a soft, slightly wet dough. Knead for 5 mins on a floured surface until smooth. Cover with a tea towel and set aside. Rising is optional for a thin crust.

    2. Time for sauce magic! Combine passata, basil, and crushed garlic, and give it a little seasoning love. Let it chill at room temperature while you work your doughy wonders on the base.
    3. Let's get rollin'! If your dough has risen, give it a quick knead, then divide it into two portions. On a floured surface, roll each piece into thin, 25cm-wide circles using your rolling pin. Carefully transfer these dough discs onto two floured baking sheets – they're ready for the pizza party!

    4. Get ready to bake! Preheat your oven to a blazing 240°C (220°C fan/gas 8) with an extra tray on the top shelf. Spread sauce, cheese, tomatoes, olive oil, and seasoning on your pizza bases. Place one pizza (still on its sheet) onto the hot tray and bake for 8-10 minutes until crispy and golden. Finish with a drizzle of olive oil and, if you like, fresh basil leaves. Repeat for the second pizza, and you're set to enjoy!
